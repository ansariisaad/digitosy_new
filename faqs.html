  • Other Questions

    1. How do I get the error log?

    You can easily retrieve the error log within Digitosy by navigating to the "Settings" or "Support" section of the software. Under the troubleshooting tab, there is an option to download or view recent error logs.

    This feature is useful for diagnosing system issues or sharing detailed reports with our support team to expedite resolution.

    2. I forgot my folder password, what should I do?

    If you forget your folder password, you can reset it through the account management system. Typically, this involves verifying your identity through email or security questions.

    1. Once verified, you’ll be able to reset the password.

    2. If the folder contains sensitive data, we recommend creating a stronger password and enabling additional security features like two-factor authentication.

    3. Limitations of folder password protection

    Folder password protection in Digitosy ensures basic security, but it’s important to note that it may not offer complete protection against more advanced intrusion methods. For stronger security, especially for critical data, we advise encrypting your files or using advanced security options provided in the system. Regular updates to passwords and other security measures are recommended to ensure data protection.

    4. Import existing library

    You can import your existing library of files, data, or assets into Digitosy through the "Import" feature located in the main dashboard. The system supports various formats, making it easy to integrate previously stored data into the Digitosy environment.

    This helps users transition their work and ensure continuity without losing any valuable data during the switch.

    5. I lost my license. Can you send it to me?

    Yes, if you’ve lost your Digitosy license, you can contact our support team for assistance.

    After verifying your purchase details, we will resend the license to the email associated with your account. It’s essential to keep your license details in a secure location to avoid future loss or delays.
