Privacy Policy

This policy is effective as of 11th November 2022

1. Introduction

At Digitosy, your privacy and trust are our top priorities. We are committed to being transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data. This Privacy Policy aims to provide clear, detailed information about the types of personal and non-personal data we gather, how we utilize that information, and the measures we take to ensure its protection. Whether you’re interacting with our website, mobile applications, or other services, we ensure that your privacy rights are respected in accordance with the latest data protection regulations, including GDPR and other applicable privacy laws

We are dedicated to giving you control over your personal information, including options to manage or delete your data. Furthermore, we continuously evaluate and update our security measures to protect against unauthorized access, breaches, or misuse. By choosing Digitosy, you entrust us with your data, and we take this responsibility seriously, working diligently to maintain your trust.

When you sign up for Digitosy, we ask for basic information such as your name, contact details, and billing information to set up your account and manage payments.

  • Additionally, when you engage in activities such as contacting customer support or filling out surveys, we collect the information you voluntarily provide.

  • We collect both personal and non-personal information to improve our services and provide a seamless experience for our users.

  • This includes direct information you provide when creating an account, such as your name, email address, and billing details, as well as data collected automatically through your interaction with the platform, like IP addresses, browser types, and usage patterns

We use various technologies, such as cookies and tracking pixels, to automatically collect information about your interactions with our platform. This includes details about your device, browser, geographic location, and how you navigate the site.

This data helps us enhance your user experience by personalizing content, improving site performance, and ensuring the platform’s security.

2.3. User comments and content

In some cases, we may collect information about you from third-party sources. This could include data from social media platforms, payment processors, or public databases.

3. Types of Information

At Digitosy, we collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. This includes personal information that you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and payment details when you sign up or make a purchase.

We also gather automatic data, like usage patterns and device information, to enhance user experience and optimize our platform. This multifaceted approach ensures we understand our users better and can deliver a more personalized and secure experience. Your privacy is paramount, and we are committed to safeguarding all types of information we collect.

At Digitosy, we utilize personal information in various ways to enhance communication and improve user experience. In the context of emails and contact forms, Digitosy collects personal information to facilitate effective communication with users. When you reach out through our contact forms or provide your email address, we use this data to respond to your inquiries, provide support, and send relevant updates.

5. Price and Taxes

  • At Digitosy, our pricing is designed to offer value while ensuring transparency. The cost of our services is clearly stated on our website, and any applicable taxes are calculated at checkout based on your location.

At Digitosy, we prioritize the privacy and protection of children's information. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a child, we will take immediate steps to delete it.
